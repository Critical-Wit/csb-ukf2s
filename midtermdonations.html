Donations

FOPAL is open for drop-off donations, no appointment needed, most weeks Monday-Saturday 3-5pm at our Main Room! Donations are not accepted the week preceding the sale; for this month and next, that means donations will not be accepted Sunday October 3 through Monday October 11, nor Sunday November 7 through Sunday 14 November.

Friends of the Palo Alto Library/FOPAL are located at 4000 Middlefield Rd. in the Cubberley Community Center/CCC. You’ll be delivering your books at FOPAL’s Main Room, the portable by the tennis courts using the CCC north entrance.

Donors will be greeted outside FOPAL’s Main Room by a volunteer Donation Monitor(s) and asked to place donations in FOPAL’s banker boxes and then move the books/boxes up the ramp to FOPAL’s Sorting Room. Sorting Room volunteers will help direct donors as to where to place the boxes and offer a receipt and a thank-you. There will be a limit of six bags/boxes per donation.

No ephemera, electronics, art, records, music CDs, or plushies/stuffed animals will be accepted. See the temporary list of what we do and do not accept at https://www.fopal.org/donate.

FOPAL offers the use of hand trucks & carts if needed.
