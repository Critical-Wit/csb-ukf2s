Directions

Driving Directions to FOPAL’s Main Book Sale Room:

  1. Turn into the North entrance of Cubberley from Middlefield Rd. (next to Charleston Shopping Center/Piazza’s).
  2. Proceed to the rear of the campus/follow the one-way arrows through the parking lot.
  3. We’re in the portable building, at the rear of the Cubberley campus, next to the tennis courts.
  4. To drop off donations, you can park close to the entrance of the portable.

Driving Directions to FOPAL’s Bargain Room (Rooms H2 & H3):

  1. Turn into the North entrance of Cubberley from Middlefield Rd. (next to Charleston Shopping Center/Piazza’s).
  2. After passing the first parking lot on the left, the Bargain Room is on the left in the first block of buildings.

Driving Directions to FOPAL’s Children’s Book Sale Room:

  1. Turn into South entrance of Cubberley from Middlefield Rd.
  2. Proceed on road through large parking lot.
  3. Pass freestanding auditorium on right.
  4. With Greendell Elementary School (now Preschool Family) on your left, turn right around building.
  5. Children’s Book Sale Room is in portable building; behind it is the Cubberley track.
  6. Park in the parking lot surrounding the portable building or proceed around next corner to large Cubberley parking lot.
